About SBPTS

A lightweight web tool created to help schools track behaviour and academic progress in a structured way.

Teachers spend less time on manual registers and more time teaching, while principals get a clear picture of every class before meetings or inspections. The system is simple enough for everyday use but detailed enough for serious reporting.
